Meet Your Tutor

Hi, I'm Sylwia Łachut.

I've always known that teaching was my calling. As a little girl, I loved to imagine I was a "teacher", and today that dream has become my career!

With over 10 years of tutoring experience (including time as a former school Maths teacher), I specialise in supporting GCSE and A-level students to build real confidence and achieve excellent results in Mathematics. I've helped students in both the UK and Poland through clear, personalised, and engaging lessons that make complex topics feel manageable and even enjoyable.

Every student is different, so I tailor lessons to fit your interests and circumstances—whether that’s connecting algebra to a favourite hobby, using everyday examples, or creating targeted practice that matches your learning style. I’m friendly, honest, hardworking, and fully focused, and my supportive, proactive style has earned consistently positive feedback from students and parents alike.

If you're looking to overcome challenges, boost confidence, and see those grades rise, I'd love to work with you (or your child)!

My Teaching Philosophy

"Master crawling before walking, and walking before running."

I believe Maths skills develop just like a child learning to move: you must master crawling before walking, and walking before running. Skipping steps only leads to frustration—so I always start with strong foundations, then guide students step by step towards fluency and independence.
